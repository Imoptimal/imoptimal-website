Custom WordPress Musical Theme

Published: 19/05/2018 | Last updated: 31/07/2020 Author: Ivan Maljukanović Categorized as: My Work Tags: wordpress theme

Description: Responsive website made for a musical group.

Challenge: Creating a multimedia rich website, with one clear goal in mind – to funnel their online traffic from all of the social media they used (Facebook, Instagram, Youtube), and to establish online presence for their production company. Design and functionality of the website based on my own original idea, with the color scheme complementing their existing branding efforts.

Demo URL: https://imoptimal.github.io/wordpress-musical-theme/

Status: Cancelled;

Note: At the end of the development process, it was cancelled. It was remade into a one page website with a musical theme.
