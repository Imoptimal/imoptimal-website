That's why I know the struggles of multitasking in your daily life - from the different hats you need to wear at work, to an equally complicated personal life. While I'm proud to say that I'm a coder, I don't just create a website - I help you achieve desired business goals. Just have in mind that a successful website doesn't happen overnight. Even though there can never be enough data, and each piece of it should be analyzed carefully - it's always better to make informed decisions, than blind guesses. The data collected on your small business website is your ownership, and you can check it freely whenever needed.

The values I stand behind:

  • I believe you should be yourself in life and business, not just what others expect from you. If you can't be authentic in your everyday life, you'll be miserable.
  • There's no point in creating business that's just another obligation in your life, and brings you no joy. You have the freedom to organize your business and life as you want, not according to other people's rules.
  • It's not only a good business decisions to optimize your website (because it converts better). Faster websites result in a more sustainable web - by using less resources and leaving a smaller carbon footprint.
  • Likewise, making your website more accessible doesn't only increase it's conversion, but it helps build more inclusive online spaces for otherwise marginalized social groups.
  • Choosing open-source tools over proprietary software (when possible) makes you more independent in the future business decisions. That way you're not limited by the choices of another business.
