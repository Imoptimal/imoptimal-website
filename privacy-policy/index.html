What personal data we collect and why we collect it

This website collects personal data only through the consultation scheduling form by Calendly (which consists of your name, email, website and preferred contact app information). This data will not be available to anybody else but me, and will not be used for any other purpose (data processing, promotions/spamming).

How long we retain your data

Personal data from the consultation scheduling form is stored until you request it to be deleted. Unfortunately Calendly still provides no option to delete any data manually or on set schedule. They only delete data after getting notified, as explained on their website in regards to GDPR compliance. If you require this data to be deleted, contact me through the form below:

Data submitted through the form above is deleted automatically after 7 days.

Cookies & Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics tools in order to track the following data: number of pageviews and sessions, average session duration, bounce rate, new vs. returning visitors, types of devices used to visit the website, referral websites, and countries from where the visitors access the website.
The mentioned data report is used by me in order to improve the content of this website.

This website also uses CloudFlare CDN in order to deliver the website faster, regardless of the visitors location.

Both of these services use their own functional cookies, and their data usage is beyond my control and responsibility.

The expiry date of your given consent is set to infinite. Only after you delete your browser cookies, you’ll be asked again for the consent.

Lastly, this website uses a cache in order to store its files on your device for a short period, and provide almost instant page load (after the initial visit when those files are downloaded).

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.
