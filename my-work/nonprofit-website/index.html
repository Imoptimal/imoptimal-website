OUR MISSION

We're building a sustainable future without the need for single-use plastics, where both the consumers and producers are making eco-friendly choices.

We believe that education is a powerful tool for change. Our organization conducts workshops, and campaigns to raise awareness about the impact of plastic pollution and to empower individuals to make a difference.

We're also collaborating with many international nonprofit organizations, in an effort to make changes worldwide.

WHY IT MATTERS?

Plastic pollution in the oceans has devastating effects on marine ecosystems.

Plastic debris can persist for hundreds of years, contributing to long-lasting environmental harm, from the highest mountain peaks to the deepest oceans.

When plastics break down, these substances can leach into soil, water, and air. Exposure to these chemicals has been linked to various health issues, including hormonal disruptions, developmental problems, and cancer.

WHAT CAN WE DO?

Use less plastic: Choose reusable items over single-use plastic (bags or water bottles).

Choose natural cosmetics: Avoid using beauty products that contain microbeads (plastic particles) that end up in our oceans.

Recycle: If you can't avoid using single-use plastic items, recycle it!
