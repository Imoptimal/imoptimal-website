Natural Homemade Cleaning Supplies

In the spirit of sustainability and health, many people are turning to homemade cleaning supplies. Not only are these alternatives eco-friendly, but they can also be made from simple ingredients you might already have at home. Here are a few recipes for natural cleaning products.

All-Purpose Cleaner

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of white vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 30 drops of essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a spray bottle.
  2. Shake well before each use.

This cleaner is great for kitchen surfaces, bathroom tiles, and other hard surfaces.

Glass Cleaner

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup of white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of rubbing alcohol
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a spray bottle.
  2. Shake well before each use.

This cleaner works well on glass surfaces and mirrors. The cornstarch helps to reduce streaking.

Toilet Bowl Cleaner

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of baking soda
  • 1/4 cup of white vinegar
  • 10 drops of tea tree essential oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl to form a paste.
  2. Apply the paste to the toilet bowl, let it sit for 15 minutes, then scrub with a toilet brush and flush.

Remember, it's always a good idea to test a small, hidden area first to make sure the cleaner won't discolor the material. Happy natural cleaning!
